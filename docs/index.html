Who We Are

About BrightNova

BrightNova Mobile Hub Services is Rubare Town's leading technology and mobile services center, proudly serving the people of Ntugamo District and beyond.

We bring together everything you need in the digital world — from finding your perfect smartphone to keeping your device running at peak performance. Our skilled technicians handle every repair with precision, while our mobile money desk ensures smooth, secure transactions.

Our HOTSPOTCENTRAL 1 WiFi hotspot keeps students, businesses, and families connected to the world at prices everyone can afford. At BrightNova, technology belongs to everyone.

We believe in honest pricing, expert service, and lasting relationships — with every single customer.
